Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of receipt. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Please ensure that the product is in its original packaging and includes all accessories.

How do I track my order?

To track your order, log in to your account and go to the "Orders" section. You can view the status of your current and past orders. Tracking information will be provided once the order has shipped.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours of placement. After this period, the order may have already been processed or shipped. To modify or cancel, please contact our customer support as soon as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Payments are processed securely using encrypted technology to protect your information.
